PHOTOGRAPHY

Photography gives me that sense of purpose. Photography also gives me creative fulfilment. It helps me express myself artistically, and that's a wonderful thing. Creativity is a major part of my life. At the end of my photo workshops, and during my seminars, I often ask the participants a simple question: “What does your photography mean to you?”
I ask the question because I know just how important photography is to many people, and I think hearing the answer out loud helps the individual and the others in the room (or around a campfire).
Yes, it’s a simple question, but one that elicits interesting—and often emotional—responses. Why the emotional response, which is sometimes accompanied by teary eyes? Because for many people, including you, photography plays an important—sometimes vital—role in life. Learn from yesterday, live for today and hope for tomorrow. The important thing is not to stop questioning. —Albert Einstein
